Dull, blank eyes stare back into yours, the light extinguished from behind them. Their emotion, conscience, morality, all dictated by nothing more than lines of code streaming directly into their prefrontal cortex.

You are looking at what mankind has become in the decades since the introduction of the cybernetic lobotomy.

During one of numerous conflicts between forgotten factions, a procedure was developed that would change humanity forever. A technology that turns an individual into a blank slate for any set of morals and ethics - the cybernetic lobotomy/prosthetic conscious - was the most incredible tool that any government could ask for. No longer did decisions have to be made difficult by emotion: soldiers could be programmed to fire at any enemy without remorse; spies would be perfectly capable of blending into even the most alien of cultures; officials didn't have to be swayed by either "good" or "bad" conscious and instead could work to the benefit of the state. The possibilities were endless.

A new kind of society

Things have changed. Cybernetic lobotomization swept every corner of society, from terrestrial urban sprawls to fringe colonial outposts. It can be found in each of the 5 major powers.

Ideas that wouldn't have ever seen the light of day have made their way into everyday life. Amazing advances, such as ubiquitous computing and advanced AI, have been corrupted in the name of the state - used to establish and perfect personalized social engineering projects on the national scale. People are molded into the perfect populace en masse without even realizing it through the manipulation of their human nature.
